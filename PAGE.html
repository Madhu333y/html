Biography

Linus Torvalds is a Finnish-American software engineer who is the creator and principle developer of linux kernel, which is kernal for a number of operating system. He also created the version control system Git. Torvalds was born on December 28, 1969 in Helsinki, Finland. He studued computer science at the university of Helsinki, Finland and he Developed the Linux kernel as a student project in 1991. Since then, the Linux Kernel has become one of the most widely used kernel in the world, powering everything from smartphones and supercomputers.
